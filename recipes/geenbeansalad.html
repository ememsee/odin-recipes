Burger

Description:

Wow! What a wonderful burger. It comes with the freshest meat available and a helping of cheese.

Ingredients:

  • beef
  • buns
  • cheese
  • ketchup
  • onions

Steps:

  1. Cook meat to desired doneness
  2. Add cheese to patty during the last portion of it cooking to melt cheese
  3. Add desired condiments
  4. Add top bun
